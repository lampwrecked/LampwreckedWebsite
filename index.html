Work

001
Signal vs. Noise

Lossy

A departure from hand-based rendering systems

From photographs to laser etching, flame to video, and finally an interactive synthesizer — each transformation of the work communicates sensory realities of inhabiting a body with chronic illness.

002
Art History Remix

Public Facing

Collages of artists who came before

Self-portraits from artists in the public domain were digitally hand cut into layers and recombined through a generative system. A long lineage of self-representation and art history torn apart and reassembled into new composites.

2025–2026↗
003
Sound Studies

Synth Sketch Demos

Browser instruments from natural systems

Interactive browser-based synthesizers based on natural systems — mycelial growth, neural meshes, live wind data, ripple physics. Each instrument is a small ecology of sound.

2026↗
004
Form Studies

Splinter Cycle

break, combine, scan, distort, combine

A broken glass sculpture scanned in 3D, manipulated as form, and projected back onto the original sculpture. The artifact and its data double held in superposition.

About

Lamp
they / them

Lampwrecked is an artist based in New York. Faced with chronic illness and fading dexterity, their conceptual practice has expanded to experiments in many mediums. A background in psychology, art history, and neuroscience informs a practice rooted in sensory processing and biological experience.

By day, Lamp is Assistant Director of Gallery Operations at Cycol Gallery in the Lower East Side.
